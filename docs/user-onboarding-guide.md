# Fireside — User Onboarding Guide

Welcome to **Fireside**, the field reporting and construction management app for South Peak. This guide walks you through everything from accepting your TestFlight invite to submitting your first ticket.

---

## Step 1 — Accept Your TestFlight Invite

1. Check your email for an invitation from **TestFlight / Apple** with the subject line *"[Name] has invited you to test Fireside."*
2. Open the email on your **iPhone or iPad**.
3. Tap **"View in TestFlight"** — this will open the App Store.

> **Don't have TestFlight installed yet?** Continue to Step 2 first, then come back and tap the link again.

---

## Step 2 — Install TestFlight

TestFlight is Apple's free app for installing beta apps. You only need to do this once.

1. Open the **App Store** on your iPhone.
2. Search for **"TestFlight"**.
3. Tap **Get** to install it (it's free).
4. Once installed, return to your invite email and tap **"View in TestFlight"**.

---

## Step 3 — Install the Fireside App

1. After opening the invite link, the **TestFlight** app will open showing the Fireside beta.
2. Tap **Accept** to join the beta testing group.
3. Tap **Install** to download the Fireside app.
4. Wait for the download to complete — the Fireside icon will appear on your home screen.

> The app may show a small orange dot on its icon — this is normal for TestFlight beta apps.

---

## Step 4 — Open the App & Create Your Account

1. Tap the **Fireside** icon on your home screen to launch the app.
2. On the sign-in screen, tap **"Create New Account"**.

### Fill in your information:

| Field | What to Enter |
|-------|--------------|
| **First Name** | Your first name |
| **Last Name** | Your last name |
| **Phone Number** | Your mobile number |
| **Email** | Your work or personal email |
| **Password** | Choose a secure password (8+ characters) |
| **Role** | Select your role (see below) |

### Choosing Your Role:

- **Owner** — Project owner / developer
- **Project Manager** — Manages units and coordinates work
- **Designer** — Interior or architectural designer assigned to units
- **Subcontractor** — Trade contractor (you'll also select your specialty)
- **Developer** — Software/tech team

If you select **Subcontractor**, you will be asked to choose your **Trade/Specialty** (e.g., Plumbing, Electrical, Framing, etc.).

3. Tap **"Create Account"** when finished.

---

## Step 5 — Wait for Account Approval

After creating your account, you will see a **"Pending Approval"** screen.

- Your account must be **approved by a project owner** before you can access the app.
- You will receive a **push notification** once you have been approved.
- This typically happens within one business day.

> **No notification?** Make sure you tapped **Allow** when the app asked for notification permissions. You can also check back by reopening the app.

---

## Step 6 — Sign In After Approval

Once approved:

1. Open Fireside.
2. Enter your **email** and **password**.
3. Tap **Sign In**.

You'll be taken through a brief **in-app walkthrough** the first time you sign in that introduces the main features.

---

## Step 7 — Explore the App

The app has five main sections accessible from the bottom tab bar:

---

### Home (Map)

- The **Home** tab shows the South Peak unit map.
- Tap any **unit** on the map to see its details and associated tickets.
- Units are labeled and color-coded based on status.

---

### Tickets

- The **Tickets** tab is where all field reports and issues are tracked.
- **To create a ticket:**
  1. Tap the **+** button.
  2. Take or select a **photo** of the issue.
  3. Choose the **building element** (e.g., Plumbing, Electrical, Framing).
  4. Set the **priority** (Low, Medium, High).
  5. Add any **notes** describing the issue.
  6. Assign team members if needed.
  7. Tap **Submit**.
- Tap any existing ticket to view details, add comments, and track status.

---

### Contacts

- The **Contacts** tab lists everyone on the project team.
- Filter by **Role** (Owner, Project Manager, Subcontractor, etc.).
- Subcontractors can be filtered by **Specialty/Trade**.
- Tap a contact card to view their details and call them directly.
- Use the **Search** bar to find someone by name.

---

### Notifications

- The **Notifications** tab shows all your alerts:
  - New tickets created on your units
  - Tickets assigned to you
  - Account approval requests (Owners only)
- Tap a notification to go directly to the related ticket.
- **Swipe left** on a notification to dismiss it.
- Unread notifications appear with an orange dot and are also shown as a badge on the tab icon.

---

### Profile

- The **Profile** tab shows your account information.
- You can update your **name**, **phone number**, and **role** here.
- Tap **Sign Out** to log out of the app.

---

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| App is stuck on loading after sign-in | Swipe the app closed and reopen it. If this keeps happening, check your internet connection. |
| Didn't receive approval notification | Ask a project owner to approve your account in the app. |
| Forgot password | On the sign-in screen, enter your email and tap **"Forgot Password?"** — a reset link will be sent to your email. |
| Can't find the app after install | Check your home screen and App Library. Search "Fireside" using Spotlight (swipe down from the middle of your screen). |
| TestFlight invite link expired | Ask to be re-invited. TestFlight invites expire after 90 days. |

---

## Tips

- **Stay signed in** — the app keeps you signed in automatically. You only need to sign in once.
- **Allow notifications** — make sure Fireside has notification permissions so you don't miss ticket updates.
- **Pull to refresh** — on any screen, pull down to refresh the latest data.
- **Updating the app** — when a new version is available, TestFlight will notify you. Open TestFlight and tap **Update** next to Fireside.

---

*For technical issues or access problems, contact your project manager or the app administrator.*
